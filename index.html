Early Life and Background

Jennifer Lynn Lopez, born on July 24, 1969, in the Bronx, New York City, is an American actress, singer, dancer, and businesswoman of Puerto Rican descent. She grew up in a working-class family and attended Catholic schools. Her parents, Guadalupe Rodriguez and David Lopez, instilled in her a strong work ethic and a love for music and dance.
